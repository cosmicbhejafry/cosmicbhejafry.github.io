zines


Every zine is readable in-browser (thanks to the EZM-reader made by Jeremy Oduber & contributors!). If you’re on a mobile device, landscape is suggested- although I don’t recommend viewing these on a mobile device.

Instructions to read (also mentioned on each page): Press “<-” or “A” to flip forward and “->” or “D” to flip backwards. Alternatively, you can click on the right half of the page to flip forward and vice versa.

I’m sorry I can’t provide alt text as of now, figuring out the best way to integrate it in the backend
