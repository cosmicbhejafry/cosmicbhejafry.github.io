This album experiments with the boundaries of genre, drawing from experiences related to gender, beauty, and the body; each track is a fortune cookie infused with the zest of pop, the warble of electronic, a dash of virtual insanity, and a lot of soul. Sophie's technical precision only adds to the dream-like ethos of "Oil of Every Pearl's Un-Insides" - what makes it all the more beautiful and intimate is how the songs are rooted in her life as a transwoman. Please listen to this brilliant album in memory of Sophie's untimely demise.
