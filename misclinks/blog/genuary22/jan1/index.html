Jan 1: draw 10,000 of something

What’s up? The first thing that came to my mind when I read the prompt and saw the number 10,000 was…Up! (you’d be barking up the wrong tree if you aren’t prepared for ‘up’ jokes)

It’s an animated Disney film, and a personal pick-me-up. ‘Up’ is about a cute kid who’s a Wilderness Explorer looking for an “Assist the Elderly” badge. This quest takes him on a sky-high trip to paradise that involves 10,000 balloons. The film is laced with heartwarming quotes…and these became the fodder for my Jan 1 code (written in p5.js).

So, yes- I drew 10,000 ascii characters. I picked up a few quotes I loved from ‘Up’, arranged the corresponding characters (adding a few spaces wherever required) into a balloon shaped outline. Hope this was your Up of tea!

the chosen ones: “adventure is out there”,“i have just met you & i love you “,“send you a post card from para dise falls “,“wilderness must be explored “,“can I come in sir? “,“kevin is a girl?! “,“else the tigers will come and eat you “

7 phrases (== 200 characters) and 350 balloons later…here’s a GIF of the final sketch. Embedding it made everything laggy, so you can play around with the sketch here. Pressing the UP arrow key fills the balloons with the corresponding text colours, pressing the DOWN arrow key removes this fill.
